Lisa Hines

Teacher, Counsellor, and Learner

Hello! I am a teacher by training with experience teaching in K-12 schools in Canada and Cambodia, as well as an after-school learning environment. My areas of speciality are Computer Science and Math, and my classes include Game Design and Robotics.

Currently, I am training to be a Marriage and Family Counsellor at Tyndale Seminary in Toronto Ontario. My desire is to help others become the best versions of themselves.

I love learning. My current interests vary from art to languages, with studies in ancient Hebrew and theology, and I love to cycle and bake!
